Our Team:

Founded in the summer of 2015, the Cyber Defense Team at SDSU provides its members the opportunity to be a part of a club that competes in hacking competitions, such as those provided by Major League Hacking.

The team provides a unique medium for its members to expand their knowledge involving the preservation of confidentiality, integrity ,and availability of information. Instruction is provided by information security professionals, as well as students who have experience in multiple fields of the cyber security industry. We also have a wide collection of computer security literature for our members to utilize. The computer security techniques taught will be tested in an isolated and controlled environments.

No prior cyber defense experience is required for team membership, our club is always looking to expand and will welcome all who are interested.
